Airbnb, Cabins, and Glamping

For all you folks who like the woods, but still require the comforts 5000 years of civilization has bestowed upon us, there are options for you as well.

Mountain Creek Cabins

The Mountain Creek cabins features hot tubs, kitchens, A/C, DVD, Television with Dish Network, electric log fireplace, full bathroom, local pizza delivery, fire pit and back deck with a gas grill, facing the woods and the mountain creek for peace and solitude. Cabins can sleep up to four. Now features Wifi!

Tentrr Glamping Tents

Located within Cooper's Rock State Park, each Tentrr campsite is equipped with a pre-set sturdy canvas tent on it's own wooden deck platform. The tent is furnished with a queen-size memory foam mattress, side tables, and a propane tent heater. A picnic table, fire pit with a grill, a solar shower, and portable camp loo are also provided, and campers are able to use the park's restroom facilities as well. There is an additional pop up tent provided for extra guests, but you will have to bring your own air mattresses/sleeping pads for this tent.
